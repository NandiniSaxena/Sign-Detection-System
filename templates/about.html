Our Mission

The Sign Language Digit Recognition project aims to bridge communication gaps by leveraging artificial intelligence to recognize sign language digits in real-time. Our goal is to create an accessible, user-friendly tool that empowers individuals to learn and interact with sign language effortlessly.

Using a convolutional neural network based on MobileNet, our system processes live camera feeds to detect and classify hand gestures representing digits 0–9. This project showcases the potential of AI in fostering inclusive communication.

Technology Stack

  • TensorFlow: Powers the MobileNet-based model for gesture recognition.
  • Flask: Serves the web interface and handles real-time predictions.
  • OpenCV: Processes live camera feeds for gesture detection.
  • Tailwind CSS: Provides responsive, modern styling.
  • JavaScript: Enables dynamic UI updates and interactivity.
